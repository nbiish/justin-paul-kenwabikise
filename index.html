About Justin Paul Kenwabikise

Justin Paul Kenwabikise is a software engineer and AI researcher building at the frontier of autonomous systems. His work spans the design and deployment of autonomous AI agents, large language model infrastructure, and post-quantum cryptographic protocols. He writes Python and Rust, architects distributed agent orchestrators, and ships production systems that integrate LLMs with real-world tooling.

Justin Kenwabikise's research interests include multi-agent coordination, secure enclave computation, and cryptographic integrity for AI inference pipelines. He contributes to open-source projects under the handle nbiish and publishes tools, frameworks, and experimental agents on GitHub.
